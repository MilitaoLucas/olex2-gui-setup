Sucrose 2

Before the tutorial begin, there are two things have to do:

 

1.Type 'reset' (Every thing will be back to original)
2.Type 'solve' (Show number of strong Q peaks)

The process of solving simple structure molecule, sucrose 

 

  • Click on the strong(bright) Q peaks

 

  • Right-click on any selected atom>Type>Carbon (All the Q peaks are assumed as carbon and reasonable bonded by computer)

 

  • Click on or type 'refine'  (The size of atoms may be different to each other which due to the actual electron density of the atoms)

Note:

          Strong(bright) Q peaks are always first choice.

Some functional keys:

  • Left-click (and hold) on the background of the main window. Moving the mouse while holding the left mouse button will move the structure
  • Right-click on an atom. A small menu will appear. Various options are available from this 'context menu'
  • Right-click on the background and explore the various options available.
