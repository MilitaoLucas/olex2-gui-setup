Data are accepted either in conjunction with publication of crystal structures or as personal communications to the Cambridge Structural Database.All data deposited with the CCDC prior to publication will be held securely in the CCDC Supplementary Data Archive, and in the case of participating publishers will be made available to referees as part of the publication process. The CCDC deposition numbers supplied should be included in the manuscript.Following publication, deposited CIF files will be transferred to the CCDC CIF Archive for public access and processed for distribution with the Cambridge Structural Database.
